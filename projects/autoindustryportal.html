The Redline

Over the course of a year, I worked with a small team of students to create a product for an outside client for our Junior Capstone. We worked with John Pecak to create "The Redline", a mobile application for both Android and iOS that allows car enthusiast to get connected with their communities and RSVP to car show events and meetups. I was the project lead for my team and worked as a manager, designer, and programmer while also maintaining client communications.

Our team used React Native and Firebase's Firestore to build our application. We used Expo to simulate our application and demonstrate the app to our client and class. I mainly focused on the frontend implementation for our team.
