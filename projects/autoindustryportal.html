The Redline

Over the course of a year for my Junior Capstone, I worked with a small team of students to create a product for an outside client. We worked with John Pecak to create the "Auto Industry Portal", a mobile application for both Android and iOS that allows car enthusiast to get connected with their communities and RSVP to car show events and meetups. I was the project lead for my team and worked as a manager, designer, and programmer while also keeping in communication with our client.
