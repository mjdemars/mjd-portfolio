About Me

Technique

The Technique is the south's liveliest college newspaper. I often work with Technique and their head design editor to create covers for their physical and digital papers. I draw each of these pieces digitally by hand using a drawing software called Medibang on my iPad. Then I throw each cover into Photoshop/Illustrator for touch-ups and for placing the required text for each issue. Each issue of the newspaper follows a specific theme, and I give my design proposal to the editor (which could include sketches for each idea) before starting to seriously continue with a design.
