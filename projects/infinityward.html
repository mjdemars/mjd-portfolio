About Me

Modern Warfare II

During the summer of 2022, I got the opportunity to intern with the UI/UX team at Infinity Ward and help implement designs for the 2022 October release of Modern Warfare 2. I worked on miscellaneous bugs and eventually was able to own my own features on the game. To complete my tasks, I worked in Visual Studio Code coding in Lua and in the studio's proprietary UI editor. I got to see various UI principles in play with my variety of tasks. Due to the nature of the work, I was also able to talk to and collaborate with people from all over the company.

Prior to joining the UI/UX team at Infinity Ward, I had never had the chance to work with a team of designers, artists, and engineers on such a large project, and I am beyond grateful for all of the guidance I received. Through this project, I was able to grow as both a programmer and a designer through my tasks and the things I learned from everybody on the team.
