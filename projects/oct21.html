Maddalen DeMars Resume Projects About Me

October 21st

For one of my game design classes, our final project was to create a narrative game in Unity and create our own assets in Maya. I was the project lead for a team of 6 members and focused on scripting in Unity, creating many of our Maya objects, managing the project's progress, and delegating tasks to the team. We built a first-person game inspired by the 2015 game "The Park" and the classic fairytale "Hansel and Gretel". The basic premise is that the player wakes up with no memory of who they are in a mysterious magical house. Outside of the house is a simple maze with scattered clues that give the player insight into who they are and what they are doing there. Our game was built using Unity and Maya.
