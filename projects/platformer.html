A Dash of Thyme

For one of my computational media classes, our final project was to prototype and build a game from scratch. We built a platform game inspired by Nintendo Mario games, and based our game around the theme of cooking. The basic premise is that you play as a chef who is preparing for a cooking competition. You have to retrieve the ingredients for the competition by dodging rats running around the platforms and collecting cheese and bread. After completing that task, you are taken through a simple process of cooking your dish for the competition. Working with a team of four, I was given the roles of programmer and designer since I had more coding experience amongst the team. Our game was built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
