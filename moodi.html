Moodi

In spring 2023, I took a class that focused on user research and designing for human computer interaction. Every design team in the class was tasked with creating a digital product that was focused around the theme of wellness and wellbeing. During the course of this semester, I led a small team of designers to create a high fidelity prototype for a mobile application aimed to helping long-distance couples (or couples with low feelings of connectedness) to increase their emotional intelligence and decrease feelings of disconnection.

My team and I researched our user group in detail, including conducting surveys and in-person interviews, studying secondary research sources, creating our own user personas, drawing up storyboards, and etc. After researching, we created a list of potential products that could address our intended issue and ended up deciding to pursue the idea of designing a couples mood ring that would allow partners to share their emotional states in real time with each other.

We worked in Figma to create our prototypes for the product and tested our app on people who fell into our intended user-group (universtiy students aged 18-24 in long-distance relationships).
