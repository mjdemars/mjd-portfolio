Horizons: Makeathon

For this art-focused hackathon, my teammate and I decided to take advantage of the art aspect of it and combine it with our interests in fashion and sustainability. For our project, we decided to thrift-flip three items of clothing and build a Figma mockup of our app idea: Sew True. We ended up winning the sustainability category of the competition and ended up with a project we truly love.
